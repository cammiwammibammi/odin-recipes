Lasagna

Description

Delicious lasagna with layers of meat sauce, bechamel, and noodles.

Ingredients

  • 12 sheets dry lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb mozzarella, shredded
  • 8 oz parmesan, grated

Meat Sauce

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 28 oz marinara sauce
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 tbsp Italian seasoning

Bechamel

  • 3 cups milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup AP flour
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp pepper

Steps

Meat Sauce

  1. In a large skillet or Dutch oven over medium-high heat, add olive oil. Add the italian seasoning and red pepper flakes. Saute for 30 seconds and add the onion. Continue cooking on medium-high heat until the onion is soft, 2 minutes. Add the garlic, stirring constantly for 30 seconds.
  2. Add the ground beef and brown meat over medium-high heat, breaking the meat apart into pieces with spoon. When cooked drain excess grease and transfer to food processor. Pulse several times until crumbly and nearly pureed.
  3. Return mixture to the skillet or Dutch oven and add the Marinara Sauce (recipe linked above). Stir to combine the meat and marinara sauce.
  4. Cover and allow the sauce to simmer for about 20 minutes on low heat, stirring occasionally. Add 1 cup of water if it gets too thick and continue cooking to reduce.

Bechamel Sauce

  1. Warm the milk in a saucepan or microwave.
  2. In a medium sauce pan melt equal parts butter and then the flour with the salt and white pepper.
  3. Whisk the flour and butter together and allow to simmer on low to cook the flour 1-2 minutes. The lightest (white color) roux will be slightly puffed.
  4. Gradually add the warm milk and whisk to incorporate until a smooth sauce forms on low heat. Cook several minutes, sauce will thicken. Set aside.

Lasagna Assembly

  1. In a 9×13″ baking dish add the dry lasagna noodles and cover with hot water. Allow to rest for 20 minutes. Drain into a large colander.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Grease the 9×13″ baking dish with cooking spray.
  3. Start by spreading some of the meat sauce on bottom of greased dish. Layer on top with 3 lasagna noodles or more to cover bottom.
  4. Spread more meat sauce over the noodles, followed by the Bechamel Sauce (recipe above), then mozzarella and parmesan cheeses, spreading all to the edges.
  5. Repeat this 3-4 times to use up all noodles, sauces and ending with cheeses on top.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes until top is golden brown and cheeses are bubbling. Allow the lasagna to rest for 15 minutes before serving.
